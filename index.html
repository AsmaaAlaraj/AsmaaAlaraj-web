Asmaa Alaraj

Optometric Assistant

I am a college student working on my BS in Biology at the University of California, Riverside. I am in my third year at UCR, and I plan on applying to Doctor of Optometry (OD) Programs here in Southern California. Currently, I am searching for possible opportunities in the Optometry field of work, and I hope to gain some enhancing experiences. I first gained an interest in the field of Optometry when my mother and brother told me of their personal experiences wearing glasses for the first time. It was a life changing moment for both of them, and since then I knew that I wanted to make that same impact on other people's lives.

I am currently a member of the COPE Health Scholar Program at Riverside Community Hospital. After training, as a Health Scholar I have hands on patient experiences throughout multiple departments of the hospital. Some of the experiences I have had include ambulating patients, feeding patients, taking vitals, and assisting nurses with discharges. Through this program I have learned how to professionaly interact with patients. Also, I have gained experience in communicating with the hospital team, including nurses, doctors, and imaging techs. This has allowed me to understand the roles that each staff member carries, and how important a team effort is for the success of a patient plan.

At UCR I hold a board position as Public Relations in a student orginization called Friends of Dialysis. My role as PR requires that I successfuly convey our mission and value statments to the campus community as well as the outside public. I make flyers for our meetings and volunteer events and insure that they are accesible to the public via our social media platforms and campus posting sites. Through this position, I have achieved a higher state of responsibility and time management. With tasks that impend on the roles of other board members, it is important that they are completed in a timely manner so as not to pull back on the progress of the organization as a whole. I have noticed that these learned qualities have also rolled over into certain areas of my life including my academics.

With more experiences ahead of me I look forward to attaining even more professional qualities that will help me in my future endeavors.
